About

Nebula Strike is a fast-paced space survival game where players navigate a spacecraft through dangerous asteroid fields deep in space. The objective is to avoid colliding with incoming asteroids while reacting quickly to an increasingly difficult environment as the game progresses. With smooth controls, simple gameplay, and a focus on reflexes and timing, each run challenges you to survive longer and beat your previous high score. Set against visually stunning space and nebula backgrounds, Nebula Strike offers an engaging arcade-style experience that is easy to pick up but difficult to master, making every attempt a test of skill and concentration.
